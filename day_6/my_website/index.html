Snowy Owls

Habitat

Snowy owls live primarily in the Arctic in treeless tundra areas. Due to the lack of trees, they typically perch on the ground or short posts.

Diet

  • Fish
  • Insects
  • Rabbits
  • Lemmings
  • Other small rodents and birds

Wing Span

The wing span of a snowy owl ranges from 50-60 inches.

Color

Snowy owls are white.

Life Span

The average life span for a snowy owl in the wild is 10 years.
