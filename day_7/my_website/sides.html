Roasted Cauliflower Steaks

Steak for ALL (even if you don't eat meat)! This also works well with cabbage. Recipe can be found here: allrecipes.

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 black pepper
  • 1/4 garlic powder
  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place the cauliflower onto a cutting board with the stem side facing upwards; cut vertically into 4 steaks approximately even in thickness. Arrange 'steaks' onto the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil over the steaks; season with garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Roast in preheated oven for 20 minutes, turn with a spatula, and continue to roast until soft in the middle and darkening around the edges, about 15 minutes more.

Deviled Potatoes

Two Southern classics- deviled eggs and potato salad- join forces create this tasty appetizer. To see original recipe head over to Southern Living.

  • 1 lb. petite potatoes
  • 1 tbl olive oil
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbl capers, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tsp chopped parsley
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tsp whole grain mustard
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Place potatoes in a small bowl, drizzle with oil. Sprinkle salt; toss to coat. Spread potatoes in a single layer on a bakeing sheet and bake until tender when pierced, about 40 min. Remove from oven and cool.
  2. Cut potatoes in half crosswise. Sccop out potato pulp into bowl, leaving shells intact. Place shells cut side up on baking sheet and bake in preheated oven until dry, about 10 min. Cool completely.
  3. Stir together potato pulp, sour cream, capers, parsley, dill, mustard, zest, and salt. Spoon mixture generously into each shell.
