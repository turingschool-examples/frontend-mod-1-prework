My current game recommendations

The Witcher 3 is an expansive RPG experience where you play as the white wolf, Gerlat of Rivia. Recently Netflix took on the task of transforming this game's lore into a series, which is only good because Henry Cavil is handsome. 10/10

Nuclear Throne is a rougelike top down shooter where you patrol a nuclear wasteland in search of the "nuclear throne." I can't lie, I don't actually know what that throne looks like because I'm not very good at the game. Very fun though!

Slay the spire is a mix of a rougelike and a card game similar to hearthstone. Your goal is to get to the top of the spire, where I'm certain amazing things happen if you're actually able to reach it. I'm not able to reach it, however, because I have the dexterity of a chimp.
