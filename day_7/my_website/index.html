Why You Should Visit Brazil!

When most foreigners think about Brazil, there are a few stereotypes that seem to be prety universal; carnival, pretty beaches, and beautiful people. While these sterotypes are for the most part true, there are a number of other reasons why Brazil should be at the top of your travel lists!


5 facts you probably didn't know about Brazil

  1. Brazil is the 5th largest country in the world by both area and population!
  2. Brazil shares a border with every country in South America except for Chile and Ecquador
  3. Brazil has over 7,000 miles of coastline!
  4. The name "Brazil" comes from a tree; "Brazilwood"
  5. Brazil is the only country in South America that speaks Portuguese
