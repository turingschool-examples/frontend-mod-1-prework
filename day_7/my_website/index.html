David Wooderson

Local legend

David Wooderson is a fictional character from the 1993 movie Dazed and Confused. He is portrayed by actor Matthew McConaughey.

David Wooderson grew up outside of Austin, Texas. An archetypal local legend he hangs out with highschoolers even though he already graduted. He works for the city but is thinking about going back to school because thats where all the girls are, right?

Wooderson is concerned with few things

  • Smoking joints
  • Drinkin beers
  • Chasing women

Say, man, you got a joint?
