Creative Wizard

My identity is tightly wrapped around the 'artist' archetype. Everything I do, every word I say, every iota of my existence is fueled by a need to be creative. I've mostly specialized in illustration and animation. I really enjoy imbuing my art with humor and capturing unique aethetics. I'd like to show some of my work to give you an idea.

"Dissatisfied Vegetables"

This is a group of dissatisfied vegetables. ...need I say more?

Tools used:
  • Copic Markers
  • Hammermill 100lb. Paper
  • Pigma Micron Technical Pens

"Swinging Them Hips"

This was my first experiment with using a tool in Adobe After Effects called "Rubberhose." This tool allows you to create limbs and adjust the way they react to movement. This little guy is breaking a heavy sweat swinging them hips around and around and around...

Tools used:
  • Adobe After Effects
  • Rubberhose (AE plugin)
  • Procreate on iPad Pro, to sketch the design out.
  • Adobe Color (website with endless color pallettes)

"Deadpool"

Here we have a little Deadpool fanart. I actually made this for a previous coworker who is OBSESSED with Deadpool. I honestly didn't expect it to come out so good, and when I viewed the finished product I really didn't want to give it to her (but I did).

Tools used:
  • Copic Markers
  • Hammermill 100lb. Paper
  • Pigma Micron Technical Pens
  • White gel pen
