What I'm passionate about:

My personal health and fitness:

Over the past couple of years I have been working toward my personal health and fitness. It's something I'm very passionate about because I have a family history of eating disorders. I too have expereinced body dysmorphia which I've overcome with a passion of weightlifting and health. I don't compete in fitness competions for weightlifting, to avoid getting into the same body-focused mindset.

Fitness for all:

My journey into accepting myself has made me passionate about encourging others to also except themselves and overcome body dysmorphia. Unfortunatly with the pressures of social media many young women have a unrealistic image of what they should look like. Many women are even turning to plasic surgey at a young age to fit in with what they see on social media.

Social media has had an huge impact on young woemen and men, who compare themselves to influencers. The article Faking it: how selfie dysmorphia is driving people to seek surgery illistrates how much of an impact this can have on young people. Read the full article here.
