DENVER DISC GOLF

Johnny Roberts Memorial DGC

Holes are very short and play through a nicely manicured park. Many new features have been added to improve the look and feel of this course. Pedestrian traffic should now be at a minimum. This is a pitch and putt style of course.

  • Address: 8001 W 59th Ave, Arvada, CO 80004
  • Holes: 18
  • Established: 1978

Bird's Nest DGC

Combination of wide open to cottonwoods. Some elevation and high pasture grass. Shoes and long pants recommended (prickly pear cactus and yuccas). 27 holes total with seasonal holes A, B, and C.

  • Address: 18125 64th Ave Arvada, CO 80403
  • Holes: 24
  • Established 2004

Badlands DSC

Very good course offering 3-4 pin placements per hole. Many elevation changes. Mostly open but trees have been planted to change that. The additional course known 'Blair Witch' is no longer there except for the few holes shared with the new course design.

  • Address: 2518 W 90th Ave, Federal Heights, CO 80260
  • Holes: 18
  • Established: 1999

Westminster DGC "White Hawk"

Water hazards (lake and river) and elevation changes in Open Space area. Trees will also be planted to enhance difficulty. Two to three possible pin placements per hole.

  • Address: Westminster City Park Circle Trail Westminster, CO 80020
  • Holes: 18
  • Established: 2003

Midway DGC

Open - down a narrow, grassy open space park and back. Big trees not much in play, but demands accuracy with private yards on one side and parking lots/businesses on the other. Normally was played basket to basket, but a local added red spray paint for alternate tees which is used more often now.

  • Address: 70 Garden Center, Broomfield, CO 80020
  • Holes: 9
  • Established: 2005

East Interlocken

Visible from highway 36, this small 9 hole course dwells in and around East Park off the Interlocken Business Loop. Short and mostly open. Alternate tee pads shown by the "#A" white spray paint. Some holes with water in play.

  • Address: 70 Garden Center, Broomfield, CO 80020
  • Holes: 9
  • Established: 2005

Centennial Park DGC

The Longmont Play it Again Sports pays $2 per disc for discs recovered from the creek with names and numbers on them. They keep the discs at the store behind the counter and if you find one of yours they only charge the $2 they paid to get your disc back.

  • Address: 1101 Lashley St, Longmont, CO 80504
  • Holes: 9
  • Established: 2009

Benedict Park DGC

Trees on some holes and some water hazards. Shared park (soccer, baseball, etc.). Concrete paths, skate park, shelters and fence near medical center are OB. Mostly flat approaches with some hills. Many mature trees. Main rules sign and map/scorecards available near hole one. Benches and trash cans throughout the course.

  • Address: 1855 E Southern St Brighton, CO 80601
  • Holes: 9
  • Established: 2008

Exposition Park DGC

Beautiful, flat park with lots of water hazards from 2 ponds and small stream. Nicely manicured grass and spacious cement tee pads. Walkways OB. Bug spray helpful in summer. Prone to flooding - keep in mind after rain and snowfall.

  • Address: E Exposition Ave, Aurora, CO 80012
  • Holes: 18
  • Established: 2004

How would you rate this course?

Green Valley Ranch DGC

Plays in and around a mostly wide open multi-use public park, with small trees and a few man-made obstacles throughout. Front nine plays around the park's south side on the mowed grass and walking paths, while back nine is laid out primarily across a natural grassland / flood plain area on the north end.

  • Address: 4900 Himalaya Rd, Denver, CO 80249
  • Holes: 18
  • Established: 2012

How would you rate this course?

David A Lorenz Park "Lighthouse"

Pretty wide open. Longer holes than most local courses. Some elevation. Multiple pins that are moved often. Brush is high in spring/summer making it difficult to find errant shots. Wear shoes or boots because you are playing disc golf. 10 new locations and practice basket added in 2016.

  • Address: 8422 S Colorado Blvd, Highlands Ranch, CO 80126
  • Holes: 18
  • Established: 2003

  • Address: 7676 S Continental Divide Rd, Littleton, CO 80127
  • Holes: 9
  • Established: 1978
