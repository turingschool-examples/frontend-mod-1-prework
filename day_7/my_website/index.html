Caddying at Cherry Hills Country Club

My Background

I used to play golf in middle school and high middle school. But stopped to focus on hockey. Eventually I found myself in Denver needing a job. Thus, how I stumbled upon being a caddie at Cherry Hills!

I have been a caddie at Cherry Hills Country Club for the past 3 summers.

There are 4 levels of caddies.

Rookie Caddie
B Caddie
A Caddie
Honor Caddie

I started as a rookie level caddie and worked my way to an A level.
