Julie is a very good dog. She's lived all over, including Colorado, California and Wisconsin. She is quite smart, and knows many words. Reach out your hand and ask her to "shake"!

Julie is originally from New Mexico. A dog rescue group took in Julie and her mom after they were found wandering on the side of the road. My wife drove to New Mexico to adopt Julie.

Julie loves a good walk; sometimes she runs! She enjoys checking each and every tree for squirrels. Julie's other passions include food, balls and snow.
