What is D&D?

Dungeons and Dragons aka. D&D is a tabletop role playing game that was first published in 1974 by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson. The game has been through several revisions and editions, with 5th Edition being the current version. D&D is structure, yet incredibly open. There are basic rules and settings, but is infinitely playable with tons of off-the-shelf adventures. You can also create your own world with your own challenges and monsters, which is called a Homebrew. Players roll a variety of polyhedral dice based on what they are trying to do. The majority of decision making rolls are done using a 20 sided die, or d20.
