What is November Project?

November Project is a free fitness movement that began in Boston by two friends who wanted to keep each other accountable by working out during the cold winter months (beginning in November). The group quickly grew from two to hundreds, and has now expanded to 52 cities across the world, including Denver!

Our Denver community meets every Wednesday morning at either the Capitol building or Civic Center park, and every Friday morning at rotating locations around the city. Both days have a 5:30am or 6:15am workout. Just show up to not only get a great workout, but to get a great start to your day with the most supportive and fun community in Denver!

A few other cities and countries that have November Project include:

  • New Orleans, LA
  • Baltimore, MD
  • Washington D.C.
  • Philadelphia, PA
  • Austin, TX
  • Orlando, FL
  • Atlanta, GA
  • Columbus, OH
  • Vancouver, Canada
  • London, England
  • Amsterdam
