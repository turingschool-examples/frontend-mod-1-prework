Mountain Biking

One of my biggest hobbies is mountain biking. I enjoy being outdoors, traveling to new places, and the adrenaline rush of ripping through the forest on two wheels. It also provides great exercises from riding up moutnains and whipping my bike up and down on jumps.

Traveling

Another big passion of mine is traveling and exploring new places. After graduating from college and working for a year, I saved up enough money to quit my job and travel around the country for 7 months. This was such an incredible experience.

I traveled all the way from Florida heading west out to California. I spent about a month traveling up the coast of California doing as much mountain biking as possible. I made it through Oregon, taking a dip in Crater Lake and stopping through Bend to see an old friend. I drove through Washington and up to Whistler, BC to meet a good friend. After too much drinking and some amazing downhill at Whistlers bike park, we caravanned out to Jasper and Banff in Alberta. If you're interested in reading more about my travels, check out my travel blog!
