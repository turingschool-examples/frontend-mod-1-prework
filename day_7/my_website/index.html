Tim's Favorite Video Games

Why Do I Like Video Games?

I like to put myself in the world where I can do anything. Whether it is from slaying monsters to farming tomatoes in my farm, videos games allows me to fulfill any interest I have without leaving the comfort of my home. With normal life of work and personal responsibilities, video games allows me to relax when I have the free time. Video games can be addicting, but it is something that help me relax from the daily life of stress.

Types of Games I Like

There are many types of game genre I like to play and the main genre I like is role-playing games. I like to put myself in a role-playing character like a mage, swordsman, wizards, or any characters that we would never see in the real world. With role-playing games, there are many hours needed to be played to make a character stronger. This is called the grind where a character repeat many tasks in order to level-up or improve skills. This can be tedious, but it give me a sense of fulfillment when I create a perfect character that can accomplish any quest or goals.
