A Lifelong Love of Meringues

Deceptively Simple Sweets

There are many different types of meringue. They almost all use sugar and egg whites as thier base components. You can add flavorings or food coloring as you like! There are meringue frostings and pie toppings but my all time favorite is the classic French meringue. The outer shell can be satiny or matte, but it is always crisp and slightly crumbly. The interior can be soild and sweet or marshmallowy and divine. Baking these sweet treats at altitude is an added challenge as well as sometimes the eggs simply don't whip up.

With the trendiness of macarons which use meringue as the "cookie" portion of the confection, there are many resources on how to make the best meringues. This link will take you to the website for one of my favorite books on everything to do with meringues!

Best Uses For Meringues

  • As an ice cream sandwhich
  • Crumbled over ice cream
  • As decorations on cakes
  • Just as they are!
