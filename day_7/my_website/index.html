Family Matters

I cannot begin to speak about anything I am passionate about without mentioning my wife and kids. Everly is my oldest, 4 years old, and Reilly is my youngest, 2 years old. My wife and I have been together for 10 years and she holds the family together. I am infinitely happy to watch them grow and equally scared to be leaving them in Las Vegas while I finish the Turing program. In the end, though, my wife and I agree it is for the best and for a short time. Hopefully I can live somewhere a little nicer by the time they begin school.

Some of my kids favorite things to do lately are:

  • Learning
  • Keeping their mom and dad up at night
  • Being VERY loud
  • Riding their bikes


Music to My Ears

Another passion of mine is writing music a poetry. Even when the content is not that good it still provides me an emotional outlet. When I feel stressed I can usually write the anxiety away. There is something unique about music, in my opinion. It can evoke all different types of emotions and it is one thing I really find solace in. I cannot play any instruments, but they do intrigue me. I mostly write lyrics, and I almost never perform anything for anyone but close friends and family. It is enough though to have the ability to vent when I feel frustrated or sad or even happy.
