Pistachio Crusted Cod

Chopped pistachios mixed with flour, garlic powder, onion owder, parsley salt, and pepper. The cod is then crusted with this mixture and pan seared. The sides include an Italian seasoned spaghetti squash and a sesame roasted bok choy. The sauce is carrot juice reduced with whipped in butter.

Beef Wellington

The classic beef wellington. A whole tenderloin wrapped in various items. These items include prosciutto, a mushroom duxelle, a brush of dijon mustard, and puff pastry. It is first seared as to apply more flavor before the wrapping process. It is generally served with a red wine or port reduction. My personal favorite side dishes include a cauiliflower puree and honey seared tri-colored carrots.
